## ğŸ’° Affiliate Program Details

### Commission Structure
- **Tier 1**: 10% (First $10,000 in referrals)
- **Tier 2**: 12.5% ($20,000+ in referrals)
- **Tier 3**: 15% ($40,000+ in referrals)

### Eligibility Requirements
- Active wedding/event planning business
- Minimum 2 years industry experience
- Professional portfolio and references
- Completion of online application process

### Payment Terms
- Quarterly payouts (January, April, July, October)
- $500 minimum threshold
- Direct deposit preferred
- 30 days after event completion

### Performance Bonuses
- **Quarterly**: 2.5-7.5% additional commission for volume achievements
- **Annual**: $5,000 bonuses for top performers
- **Tier Achievement**: One-time bonuses ($500-$2,500) for reaching new tiers
