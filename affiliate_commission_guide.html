Program Overview

This comprehensive guide outlines the commission structure, payment terms, and incentive programs for On Cloud Nine's Affiliate Program. Designed for wedding planners, photographers, and industry professionals, this program offers competitive commissions and tiered incentives to reward your valuable referrals.

Commission Rates and Tiers

Standard Commission Structure

Our affiliate program offers competitive percentage-based commissions on confirmed bookings:

  • Base Commission Rate: 10-15% of total booking value
  • Average Commission per Referral: $1,000 - $1,500+
  • Potential Monthly Earnings: $15,000+
  • Potential Annual Earnings: $50,000+

Commission Calculations and Examples

Basic Commission Calculation

Commission = Booking Value × Commission Rate

Example Calculation

Single Referral: $10,000 booking value
Commission Rate: 10% (Tier 1)
Commission = $10,000 × 0.10 = $1,000

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When do I receive my commission payments?
A: Commissions are processed 30 days after event completion and paid quarterly on the 15th of January, April, July, and October.
Q: What's the minimum payout threshold?
A: The minimum payout threshold is $500. Earnings accumulate until this threshold is reached.
Q: How are commissions calculated for customized packages?
A: Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the final booking value, including all add-on services and customizations.
Q: Can I track my referrals and commissions in real-time?
A: Yes, you'll have access to a personalized affiliate dashboard with real-time tracking of referrals, bookings, and commission earnings.
Q: What are the requirements to join the affiliate program?
A: You need an active business in wedding/event services, minimum 2 years experience, and must complete our online application and orientation process.
Q: What marketing materials do I receive?
A: You'll receive high-quality images, promotional banners, email templates, social media posts, and customizable brochures.
Q: How do I maintain my tier status?
A: Maintain your tier by meeting the annual revenue thresholds. Tier status applies to future bookings within the calendar year.
Q: What happens if I don't meet the minimum revenue requirements?
A: You'll maintain your current tier status for the remainder of the year, but must meet the threshold again next year to maintain that tier level.
