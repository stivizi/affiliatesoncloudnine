    Commission and Payment Questions

    When do I receive my commission payments?
    Commissions are processed 30 days after event completion and paid quarterly on the 15th of January, April, July, and October.
    What's the minimum payout threshold?
    The minimum payout threshold is $500. Earnings accumulate until this threshold is reached.
    How are commissions calculated for customized packages?
    Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the final booking value, including all add-on services and customizations.
    Can I track my referrals and commissions in real-time?
    Yes, you'll have access to a personalized affiliate dashboard with real-time tracking of referrals, bookings, and commission earnings.

    Program Requirements

    What are the requirements to join the affiliate program?
    You need an active business in wedding/event services, minimum 2 years experience, and must complete our online application and orientation process.
    How do I maintain my tier status?
    Maintain your tier by meeting the annual revenue thresholds. Tier status applies to future bookings within the calendar year.
    What happens if I don't meet the minimum revenue requirements?
    You'll maintain your current tier status for the remainder of the year, but must meet the threshold again next year to maintain that tier level.

    Marketing and Promotion

    What marketing materials do I receive?
    You'll receive high-quality images, promotional banners, email templates, social media posts, and customizable brochures.
    Can I create my own promotional materials?
    Yes, but all materials must be approved by our team and follow brand guidelines before use.
    Are there restrictions on how I can promote On Cloud Nine?
    You must disclose your affiliate relationship, use only approved materials, and avoid making unsubstantiated claims about our services.

    Support and Resources

    What support do I receive as an affiliate?
    You'll have access to a dedicated affiliate manager, comprehensive marketing materials, and ongoing training resources.
    How do I get help with the affiliate program?
    Contact your dedicated affiliate manager or use our support portal for assistance with tracking, marketing materials, or program questions.
    Are there opportunities for additional training?
    Yes, we offer regular webinars, training sessions, and networking events exclusively for our affiliates.
